Simulate:
A Campfire Ring

Circular avatar chips in a horizontal strip. HP state is encoded entirely via ring colour (green / amber / red) — no numbers visible by default. Minimal vertical footprint (~70px). Works well when you just need a quick pulse check on the party.

B Party Cards

Portrait-first cards in a horizontal scrolling row. Each card shows the portrait prominently at the top, then name and an HP bar with current/max numbers. Scrolls horizontally for larger parties. Moderate footprint (~120px).

C Squad Status

Compact vertical list — portrait thumbnail, name, and HP bar in each row. Most information-dense design. Easy to scan HP at a glance. Scales cleanly to larger parties without horizontal scroll. Footprint scales with party size.
