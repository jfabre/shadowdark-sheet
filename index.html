Character Creation

Follow these steps to forge your adventurer.
Get the free Quickstart Set

  1. Name — Choose a worthy name.
  2. Background — Roll a d20 or pick a past that defines your skills.
  3. Stats — Roll 3d6 in order for STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA. Reroll all six if none are 14+.
  4. Ancestry — Dwarf, Elf, Goblin, Halfling, Half-Orc, or Human. Each grants languages and a unique trait.
  5. Class — Fighter (all weapons & armor, 1d8 HP), Priest (divine caster, 1d6 HP), Thief (stealth & backstab, 1d4 HP), or Wizard (arcane caster, 1d4 HP).
  6. Alignment — Lawful (order), Neutral (balance), or Chaotic (destruction).
  7. Deity — Choose a god to worship (required for Priests).
  8. Gear — Carry up to your STR (min 10) in slots. Start with a Crawling Kit (7 gp) and buy weapons & armor.
  9. Level Up — Earn current level × 10 XP. Talent roll at odd levels.

Help

Tips & Shortcuts

  • Portrait upload: Click the portrait area to browse files, or drag and drop an image directly onto it.
  • Autocomplete navigation: Use ↑ / ↓ arrow keys to navigate suggestions, Enter to select, Esc to dismiss.
  • AC auto-calculation: Armor Class updates automatically when you change armor or Dexterity — no manual entry needed.
  • Encumbrance & Hauler: Gear slots are tracked automatically. The Hauler talent increases your carrying capacity by adding your Strength modifier.
  • Auto-save: Your character is saved automatically to your browser after every change — no Save button needed.
  • Talent slots: Talent slots unlock as you level up. Your class determines which talents are available.
  • Themes: Open Settings → Theme to switch between visual themes. Your choice is remembered between sessions.

FAQ

My character disappeared after an update — what happened?
Browser updates can sometimes clear local storage. Always export a backup before updating your browser or clearing site data.
How do I back up my character?
Open Settings → Export Character. This copies your character data to the clipboard as JSON. Paste it somewhere safe (a text file, email, notes app).
How do I restore a backup?
Open Settings → Import Character, paste your previously exported JSON into the text area, and click Import. You will be asked to confirm before your current character is replaced.
My Armor Class changed unexpectedly — why?
AC is calculated automatically from your equipped armor and Dexterity modifier. Changing either of those values will update AC. Unarmored characters use 10 + Dex modifier.
How do talent slots work?
You gain talent slots at levels 2, 5, and beyond, as defined by your class. Each slot lets you choose one talent from your class talent table. Some talents can be taken more than once.
Can I roll dice in the browser?
Yes — click any dice icon next to a stat or skill to roll that die. Results appear in a toast notification at the top of the screen.
How do I switch themes?
Open Settings → Theme and click any swatch. The new theme is applied instantly and saved for your next visit.
